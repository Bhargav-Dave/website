## Communications Specialist

Date of Posting: 20 December 2021

## About the Position
Tattle is looking for a part time/full-time communications specialist to build Tattle's online presence. At the very least, this requires building and maintaining Tattle's social media presence but the scope will be refined based on time availability and skillset.        

Interested candidates must have some prior experience with digital marketing and/or online community organizing.   


**Start Date:** January 2022.
**Location:** Remote
**Duration:** Full-Time/ Part-time
**Remuneration:** Based on time availability and scope of work. 
**Deadline:** Rolling 

## About Tattle
Tattle is a civic tech project committed to claiming a bigger space for civic action against misinformation and hate speech in India. We're a cross-disciplinary team of engineers and researchers. We value inter-disciplinary thinking and unconventional life experiences. We provide space to incorporate unique individual learnings in all that we make. We provide a stress free work environment with a flexible 8 hours workday policy. 


We are a fully remote team that communicates asynchronously.
### We Value Diversity and Multiplicity of Experiences
At Tattle, we cherish diversity of experiences and perspectives. It adds value to our work. To this end, we strongly encourage candidates, even if they feel they don't meet all the above listed criteria but find alignment with the project and are driven to learn, to apply.
